User guide

While using this application you can use the search box to find your favourite movies and add them to your watchlist.

Pressing the Watchlist button you can view your watchlist.

To add a movie to your watchlist just click Add to watchlist button next to a movie.

To remove a movie from your watchlist just click Remove from watchlist button next to a movie.
